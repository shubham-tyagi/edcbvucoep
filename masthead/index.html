The Entrepreneurship Summit (E-Summit) is the annual flagship event of The Entrepreneurship Cell, BVDU Pune which is a student organization with its aim and mission to foster and sustain the entrepreneurial spirit among students and youth.

E-Summit is an initiative to serve as a platform for bringing together budding entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, investors, start-ups and everyone else with a spirit of entrepreneurship. It creates a networking hub for people from varied domains by involving corporations, working professionals and students from all across the nation. Thus, it gives a chance for people to interact and helps build successful ventures.
